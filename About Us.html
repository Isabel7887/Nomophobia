Who are we and Why do this?

My name is Aurora Isabel López Torres, and I am part of the NextGen Tech Scholar 2023 Program of the Society of Women Coders. One of their mission is to communicate to the world about problems that occur inside society. These could be problems that affect us or the planet. In my project, I wanted to explain to people about a social problem that is not constantly mentioned. Nomophobia and addiction to technology are affecting people. The problem is that sometimes we do not know that it even exists. Society does not recognize this as a problem, but it is changing people’s lives, and we need to know about it. That is why I created this project, to inform the world about the other side of technology, the negative part of using it. I hope this information is helpful to you and shared to make more people aware of it.
